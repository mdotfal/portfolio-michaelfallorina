ShootCast App

const skills = [React, Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript];

ShootCast is a mobile app that will help you create custom lists of cities to see weather conditions with the click of a mouse.

This application is for photographers, videographers, & other creative artists looking to plan shoots by being able to check the current weather conditions and upcoming weather forecasts.

I built this app because of the unpredictable weather conditions the S.F. Bay Area sees day to day. This solves the need to look up every city separately and creates a way to bookmark a list of favorite cities to view instantly.
